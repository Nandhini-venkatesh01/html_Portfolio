The Best Movies According to Nandhini

My top 3 best movies

Vikram

Vikram is an action thriller movie directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj. The movie stars Kamal Haasan, Vijay Sethupathi, and Fahadh Faasil in lead roles. The movie has a gripping storyline, intense action sequences, and brilliant performances by the cast. The movie's background score and cinematography are also top-notch. Overall, Vikram is a must-watch for action movie lovers.

Periyerum Perumal

Periyerum Perumal is a Tamil drama film directed by Mari Selvaraj. The film stars Kathir and Anandhi in lead roles. The story revolves around a law student from a marginalized community who faces caste-based discrimination. The film received critical acclaim for its powerful storytelling and social commentary.

Kantara

Kantara is a Kannada action thriller film directed by Rishab Shetty, who also stars in the film. The story is set in a small village and revolves around the conflict between villagers and a greedy land mafia. The film is known for its gripping narrative, strong performances, and stunning visuals.
